### **第一部分：核心准则**
Quality Assurance Statement 章节必须包含验证试验编号、验证试验名称，以及QAU检查声明。声明需确认报告准确描述试验材料、方法和原始数据（排除待测物及内标的CoA和RoA）。表格需列出检查类型、项目、日期和报告日期。若无具体检查数据，使用标准QAU审计模板，确保GLP合规性声明完整，无偏差。

### **第二部分：通用模板**
生成 Quality Assurance Statement 章节，使用以下结构：

**Quality Assurance Statement**  
验证试验编号（Validation Study Number）：{{Validation_Study_Number}}  
验证试验名称（Validation Study Title）：{{Validation_Study_Title}}  

本机构QAU对该验证进行了如下检查，本验证报告如实描述试验材料和方法，准确反映验证过程中产生的原始数据（待测物{{Analyte}}的CoA及待测物{{Analyte}}同位素内标的RoA除外）。  
The following events or documents of the study were audited by the QAU of test facility. The final validation report accurately and completely describes the materials and methods used in the study, and the results in the report accurately and completely reflect the raw data of the study (except the Certificate of Analysis (CoA) of reference standard {{Analyte}} and the Report of Analysis (RoA) of internal reference standard {{Internal_Standard}}).
